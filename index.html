Our Story

At KFC, our story began with one cook who created a finger-lickin’ good recipe more than 75 years ago. Colonel Harland Sanders believed in quality, freshness, and flavor that brings people together. From a small kitchen in Kentucky to thousands of restaurants around the world, we continue his legacy by serving freshly prepared chicken with our secret blend of 11 herbs and spices. KFC — where every meal is made with care and passion.
